bmm350_status_e bmm350_magnetic_reset_and_wait (struct bmm350_dev_s *dev)
 Performs a magnetic reset of the BMM350 sensor. This is necessary when: The sensor may have been exposed to excessively strong magnetic fields (e.g. >400 mT). Long periods of inactivity have caused capacitor discharge on the CRST pin. You're resuming from suspend mode and want to guarantee accuracy.
